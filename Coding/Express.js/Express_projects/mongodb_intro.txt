Intro of Mongodb?

MongoDB stores data in the form of documents, which are JSON-like field and value pairs.
 (e.g. dictionaries, hashes, maps, and associative arrays).like
	
	{
		name:"parkash",
		age:20,
		sex:"male"
	}

MongoDB stores all documents in collections.A grouping of MongoDB documents.
A collection is the equivalent of an RDBMS table.

for more visit:https://docs.mongodb.org/manual/core/crud-introduction/
